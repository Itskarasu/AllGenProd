ABOUT US

What is Production Ministry?

Serving God through technology. Through the use of multi-media platform, Prod team aims to provide a conducive and distraction-free experience both for our onsite and online worship services. Our goal is to help the church to clearly hear and see the truth of God’s Word and to experience Him in a creative way.
